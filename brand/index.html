Brand Identity

More than a logo — your business identity.

Your brand is how people recognize your business, remember your business, and decide whether they trust your business. A strong brand identity creates consistency across your logo, colours, typography, website, and marketing materials so your business looks professional everywhere it appears.

At Blacksmith Media, we build brand identities from the ground up — starting with direction and strategy, and finishing with a complete visual identity system that your business can use across your website, social media, vehicles, signage, print, and advertising.

What’s Included

What we build for your brand

Logo Design

Primary logo, alternate logo versions, and logo variations for different uses and layouts.

Colour System

A defined brand colour palette so your business always looks consistent and recognizable.

Typography

Font selection and hierarchy so your brand has a consistent visual style across every medium.

Brand Direction

Creative direction that shapes the tone, style, and visual feeling of the business.

Brand Guidelines

A simple guide that shows how to use your logo, colours, and fonts properly going forward.

Digital Assets

Brand files and graphics prepared for website, social media, and digital marketing use.
