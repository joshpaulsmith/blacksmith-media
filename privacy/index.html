Privacy Policy

How we handle your information

This page explains what information Blacksmith Media collects through the website and how project inquiries are handled.

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Information You Send

When you submit a project form or contact form, we collect the details you provide, such as your name, email address, business name, service interest, and message. This information is used to respond to your inquiry and discuss the work you requested.

Website Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics to understand general website activity, such as page visits and traffic sources. Analytics data helps us improve the website and understand which pages are useful to visitors.

Form Processing

Contact forms may be processed through FormSubmit. Information entered into a form is sent so we can receive and respond to your message. Do not include sensitive personal, financial, or confidential information in the form.

How Information Is Used

Inquiry information is used to respond to project requests, provide service information, and communicate about possible work. We do not sell your contact information.

Contact

For privacy questions or to request deletion of an inquiry, email blacksmithmedia@protonmail.com.
